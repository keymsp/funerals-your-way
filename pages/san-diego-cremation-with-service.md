## Frequently Asked Questions About Cremation With a Service

### Can we have a service before cremation?

Yes. Families may choose a funeral service with viewing or visitation prior to cremation, or hold a memorial service afterward.

### Is embalming required?

Embalming is only required when viewing or visitation is selected. It is not required for cremation alone.

### How soon does everything need to be decided?

There is no immediate rush. Cremation allows flexibility, and services may be held days, weeks, or even months later.

### What is the difference between a funeral and a memorial service?

A funeral service typically takes place before cremation and may include viewing or visitation. A memorial service is held after cremation and focuses on remembrance and celebration of life.

### Can we personalize the service?

Yes. Services can be personalized with music, readings, photos, video tributes, or cultural and religious elements that reflect your loved one's life.

### How much does cremation with a service cost?

Costs vary depending on the level of service, location, and personalization selected. We provide clear, upfront pricing so you understand exactly what to expect before making any decisions.
