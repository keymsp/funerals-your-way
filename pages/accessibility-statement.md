**Effective Date: March 2026**

Funerals Your Way is committed to making our website accessible to all visitors, including individuals with disabilities. We strive to ensure that our website provides an inclusive and user-friendly experience for everyone.

We make reasonable efforts to follow widely recognized accessibility standards and improve the usability of our website. Our goal is to allow visitors to easily access information about funeral services, cremation options, and memorial arrangements.

If you experience difficulty accessing any content on this website or need assistance obtaining information, please contact us and we will make every effort to assist you.
