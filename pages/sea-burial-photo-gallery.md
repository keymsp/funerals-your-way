# What a Burial at Sea Is Like

These photos respectfully show what families experience during a Burial at Sea. Each ceremony is different, but the setting is typically calm and unhurried, allowing families time to reflect, participate, and say goodbye in their own way.

[Learn more about ash scattering / sea burial](/san-diego-burial-at-sea/)

## Military Veteran Sea Scattering

Because San Diego is a military town we have many veterans that have served in various branches of the military – especially Navy. Because of this, many of them are drawn toward having a Burial at Sea.

![Bugler for a Burial at Sea Service](https://funeralsyourway.com/wp-content/uploads/buggler-for-burial-at-sea-320-cp.jpg)

On occasion, we have a live bugle player on board.

![Military Honors before a burial at sea](https://funeralsyourway.com/wp-content/uploads/military-honors-before-burial-at-sea-cp.jpg)

**Military Honors** being given to a veteran before boarding the yacht. Additional information: [Military Honors](/veteran-military-honors/)

![Returning to port from a burial at sea with the flag in the foreground and a military ship in the background](https://funeralsyourway.com/wp-content/uploads/burial-at-sea-military-ship-background-flag-cp.jpg)

Returning to port from a Burial at Sea off of Point Loma. The flag flying in the foreground with a military ship in the background.

## Sunsets at Sea

Sunsets can be spectacular on the ocean and these sea burial photos give you some idea. Many families do select this time of day for their event. If the sky is clear you may even see the green flash.

![Sunset at Point Loma after a sea burial](https://funeralsyourway.com/wp-content/uploads/sunset-at-point-loma-after-sea-burial-cp.jpg)

![Sunset near Point Loma after a scattering at sea](https://funeralsyourway.com/wp-content/uploads/sunset-on-ocean-point-loma-cp.jpg)

Beautiful sunsets off Point Loma.

![Burial at sea at sunset](https://funeralsyourway.com/wp-content/uploads/burial-at-sea-sunset-ocean-cp.jpg)

Burials at Sea in the evening or in the morning are **particularly spectacular**.
