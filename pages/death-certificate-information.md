Please accept our **sincere sympathies on losing your loved one**. At this challenging time, we do our best to have the arrangement process flow smoothly for the family. With this in mind, you may complete the following form to supply us with the information we will **require for the death certificate**. If you desire, we can also fax or email the required documents to you for your signature.

If you have any questions, please **do not hesitate** to contact us at [619-550-7807](tel:+16195507807) or [contact us online](/contact-us/).

## Decedent Personal Information

The following information is needed about the deceased:

- First Name
- Middle Name
- Last Name
- AKA / Also Known As
- Date of Birth
- Age (years)
- Sex
- Birth State / Foreign Country
- Social Security Number
- Date of Death
- Hour of Death (24-hour format)
- Education (highest level)
- Usual Occupation
- Kind of Business or Industry
- Years in Occupation
- Decedent's Race

## Decedent Usual Residence

- Street Address
- City, State, ZIP Code
- Years in County (if less than 1 year, specify 0)
- Was there military service? (Yes / No)

### Military Service Information (if applicable)

- Branch of Service
- Have discharge papers (DD214)? (Yes / No)

## Marital Status

- Married
- Divorced
- Never Married
- Widowed
- Other

### Marriage Information (if applicable)

- Spouse Name
- Spouse Maiden Name

## Informant — Person Completing This Form

- First Name
- Last Name
- Relationship to Decedent
- Street Address
- City, State, ZIP Code
- Phone
- Email

## Parents

- Name of Father (first, middle, last)
- Birth State of Father
- Name of Mother (first, middle, last / maiden)
- Birth State of Mother

## Additional Information & Service Preferences

- Any additional information or wishes regarding funeral services
- **Disposition Choice:**
  - Cremation / Residence
  - Cremation / Burial in Cemetery
  - Cremation / National Cemetery
  - Casket Burial
  - Body Transfer
  - Unsure
- **Number of Death Certificates Needed** ($26 each)
- **Type of Services Interested In** (multiple selections allowed):
  - 15 minute viewing
  - 1 hr Service
  - 2 hr Service
  - Burial at Sea 1–6 people
  - Burial at Sea 7–12 people
  - Burial at Sea 13 or more people
  - No service
