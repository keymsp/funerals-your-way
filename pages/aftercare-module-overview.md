Losing a loved one can feel overwhelming. Beyond the service itself, families are often faced with important decisions, paperwork, and responsibilities that continue in the days and weeks that follow.

Our **Aftercare Program provides clear, step-by-step guidance** to help families navigate everything that follows a loss — from immediate next steps to legal, financial, and administrative responsibilities.

- **Step-by-Step Guidance** — Clear instructions on what to do first, what can wait, and how to move forward without feeling overwhelmed.
- **Legal & Administrative Support** — Guidance on death certificates, Social Security, financial accounts, and required notifications.
- **Checklists & Templates** — Downloadable checklists, forms, and templates designed to save time and prevent costly mistakes.
- **Ongoing Support** — Access to continued guidance and answers in the weeks and months following the service.

Below, you'll find an overview of each module. You may "Read Summary" for a quick introduction. **Full access to the complete program and downloadable resources is provided to the families we serve.**

![aftercare 2](https://funeralsyourway.com/wp-content/uploads/aftercare-2.webp)

![aftercare 3](https://funeralsyourway.com/wp-content/uploads/aftercare-3.webp)
