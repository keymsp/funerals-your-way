At Funerals Your Way, we are **honored to serve those who have served our country**. We understand that navigating veterans' benefits and memorial options can be overwhelming during a time of loss. This **guide was created to help veteran families** access the most important forms, government resources, funeral honors, and supportive organizations available to them. Whether you're applying for burial benefits, seeking grief support, or planning a meaningful tribute, this **comprehensive list will help you every step of the way**. If you need personal assistance, our team is here to guide you with care and respect.

Browse the sections below to find the forms, resources, and support you may need.

- **VA Forms & Applications** — Essential forms for burial benefits, headstones, flags, and memorial certificates.
- **VA & Government Resources** — Official websites for benefits, healthcare, cemeteries, records, and VA services.
- **Funeral & Memorial Support** — Military honors, buglers, wreaths, memorial certificates, and veteran tribute resources.
- **Support for Veterans & Families** — Grief support, counseling, mental health help, and family assistance programs.
- **Community Nonprofits** — Organizations offering practical aid, programs, and support for veterans and families.
- **Veteran Service Organizations** — Advocacy and claims-support groups such as The American Legion, VFW, DAV, and AMVETS.

If you're unsure where to start or need help with any forms or benefits, call [619-550-7807](tel:+16195507807) to speak with a licensed funeral director anytime.

Links to external websites do not imply endorsement or responsibility for their content.

## VA Applications & Essential Forms

| Resource | Description | Link |
|---|---|---|
| Application for Burial Benefits | Reimbursement form for burial and funeral costs for eligible veterans. (VA Form 21P-530) | [View Form](https://www.va.gov/find-forms/about-form-21p-530/) |
| Application for Headstone or Marker | Request for a government-furnished headstone, marker, or niche plaque. (VA Form 40-1330) | [View Form](https://www.va.gov/find-forms/about-form-40-1330/) |
| Application for American Flag | Request for a U.S. flag to drape the casket or accompany the urn. (VA Form 27-2008) | [View Form](https://www.va.gov/find-forms/about-form-27-2008/) |
| Application for Presidential Memorial Certificate | Certificate signed by the President to honor the memory of an honorably discharged veteran. (VA Form 40-0247) | [View Form](https://www.va.gov/find-forms/about-form-40-0247/) |
| Request for Military Record | Order discharge documents (DD214) or service records. | [Visit Site](https://www.archives.gov/veterans) |
| Application for Medallion | For privately purchased headstones: request a VA medallion for veterans. (VA Form 40-1330M) | [View Form](https://www.va.gov/find-forms/about-form-40-1330m/) |
