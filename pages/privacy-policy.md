**Effective Date: March 2026**

Funerals Your Way respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you provide when visiting this website or contacting us regarding funeral, cremation, or memorial services.

This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and protected when you interact with our website.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Voluntarily Provide

When you contact us through forms, email, phone, or service inquiries, we may collect information such as:

1. Name
2. Email address
3. Phone number
4. Address
5. Information related to funeral or cremation arrangements
6. Information provided during service planning inquiries

This information is used only to respond to your inquiry, assist with funeral arrangements, or provide requested information about services.

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

### Information Collected Automatically

Like most websites, certain technical information may be collected automatically when you visit the site, including:

- IP address
- Browser type
- Device type
- Pages visited
- Time spent on pages
- Referring websites

This information helps us understand how visitors use the website and allows us to improve the user experience.

## Cookies

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to improve website performance and analyze visitor activity.

Cookies are small files stored on your device that allow websites to remember certain preferences and improve functionality.

You may disable cookies through your browser settings, although some parts of the website may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

## Website Analytics

We may use website analytics services to understand how visitors interact with our website. These services help us analyze general traffic patterns such as:

- Pages visited
- Duration of visits
- Approximate geographic region of visitors

This information helps us improve the website and better assist families seeking funeral and cremation services.

Analytics services do not provide personally identifiable information unless voluntarily submitted through a contact form.

## Contact Forms and Service Inquiries

When you submit a contact form or service request on this website, the information you provide is used only to respond to your inquiry or assist with funeral service planning.

Information submitted through forms may be securely stored for customer service and record-keeping purposes.

## Sharing of Information

Personal information may be shared only when necessary to:

- Coordinate funeral or cremation services
- Comply with applicable legal requirements
- Complete service arrangements requested by the family

Funerals Your Way does not sell or distribute personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

## Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect the information submitted through this website. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage method is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## External Links

This website may contain links to external resources or third-party websites for informational purposes. Funerals Your Way is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those websites.

## California Privacy Rights

California residents may have certain rights regarding personal information under applicable privacy laws. Individuals may request information regarding how personal information is used or request that certain personal information be deleted when applicable.

To submit a privacy-related request, please contact us using the information below.

## Children's Privacy

This website is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Updates to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in website practices or applicable laws. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
