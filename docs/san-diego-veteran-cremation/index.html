San Diego Veteran Cremation

Simple, Dignified Veteran Cremation — With Military Honors Included
Clear pricing. No pressure. We handle everything.

Funerals Your Way offers two veteran cremation packages designed to meet the needs of most San Diego families, with options for customization when needed. A veteran’s farewell should reflect their service with dignity and respect.

Our veteran cremation services follow the same licensed and professional cremation process as standard cremation, with additional coordination for military benefits, honors, and national cemetery interment when applicable. We handle the details with care, clarity, and respect so families are not left navigating the process alone.

What Happens When You Call

When you contact us, we guide you step-by-step:

  1. We listen and answer your questions
  2. We coordinate bringing your loved one into our care
  3. We begin paperwork and veteran benefit coordination
  4. We schedule services and military honors

You don’t need to have everything figured out—we will guide you through each step.

Veteran Cremation Packages at a Glance

  • Direct Cremation — Simple, dignified cremation with full military honors and interment coordination.
  • Cremation with Service — Viewing and funeral service before cremation for families who want time to gather.

Have questions or need help deciding? Speak with a licensed funeral director anytime — 619-550-7807

Veteran Cremation – Direct – $1,895

A simple, dignified option that includes cremation, military honors, and interment coordination at Miramar National Cemetery.

We schedule the interment at Miramar National Cemetery, organize the honor guard, and coordinate all required details for the ceremony. Columbarium (wall) or ground interment may be arranged. If ground interment is selected, a government-provided headstone is included.

This package includes all of the following:

Care & Legal Coordination

  • Taking your loved one into our care
  • Climate-controlled holding facility
  • Filing of all legal paperwork
  • Disposition permit (1 included)
  • Notification of Social Security

Cremation & Remains

  • Transfer to licensed crematory
  • Basic container for cremation
  • Cremated remains returned to the family

Veteran Honors & Interment

  • Scheduling at Miramar National Cemetery
  • Military honor guard
  • Live bugle player
  • Burial flag provided

  • Veteran urn with branch insignia and nameplate

  • Tree planted in honor of your loved one (complementary)

  • Aftercare package (complementary)

Veterans who choose not to receive the upgraded urn receive a $200 discount on this package.

Veteran Cremation – With Service – $4,595

This option is preferred by families who wish to hold a viewing and funeral service prior to cremation, allowing time to gather, say goodbye, and honor their loved one in a more traditional setting. It provides an opportunity for family and friends to come together, share memories, and find support before the cremation takes place.

This package includes everything in the Direct package, plus:

Preparation & Viewing

  • Embalming (standard)
  • Dressing, cosmetics, and hair care
  • Rental casket

Service & Transportation

  • One-hour visitation (open or closed casket)
  • One-hour funeral service following visitation

  • Funeral coach (hearse) for up to two hours

  • Tree planted in honor of your loved one (complementary)

  • Aftercare package (complementary)

Additional Information

Veteran Cremation Benefits

Funeral expenses can be significant, but eligible veterans and their families may qualify for meaningful benefits through military and veterans programs. While these benefits are not paid in advance and reimbursement can take time, they often provide substantial value and recognition.

One of the most meaningful options available is interment at a national cemetery, which provides a dignified and lasting place of honor.

We guide families through every step, including eligibility, documentation, and scheduling. You can also learn more about veteran funerals in general and how we coordinate everything on your behalf.

Most families we serve choose interment at Miramar National Cemetery, where military honors are performed in a structured, respectful setting. We handle the scheduling, coordination, and all required arrangements on your behalf.

Veteran Benefits at a National Cemetery

Eligible veterans and their families may receive a range of meaningful benefits through national cemetery interment. These benefits provide both financial relief and a dignified, lasting tribute to their service.

  • No-Cost Interment — Interment at a national cemetery is provided at no cost for eligible veterans.
  • Military Honors — Honor guard, flag presentation, and a formal tribute to recognize service.
  • Committal Shelter — A structured and respectful setting for a brief, meaningful service.
  • Headstone or Niche Cover — Government-provided marker or niche cover with name and service details.
  • Perpetual Care — Ongoing maintenance and care of the resting place at no cost to the family.

With cremation, families may choose either columbarium (wall placement) or ground interment, depending on availability and preference.
