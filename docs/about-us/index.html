Compassion, Care, and Community — About Funerals Your Way

At Funerals Your Way, we believe that every life deserves to be remembered and honored in a way that is meaningful and dignified. For families planning services after a loss or for those arranging in advance, we provide straightforward information and kind assistance. We help families make decisions that feel right for them.

Our name is our mission—Funerals Your Way. We help families build customized funeral and cremation services that honor the unique life of their loved one.

Why Families Trust Us

Families choose Funerals Your Way because they want more than a service—they want to feel heard, respected, and supported. From our licensed care and secure facilities to our clear communication and compassionate staff, every detail is handled with dignity and professionalism.

  • 200+ 5-Star Reviews — Trusted by hundreds of families
  • Secure, Dignified Facility — Secure, alarmed, and camera-monitored facility
  • Licensed Funeral Home — California FD #2188
  • Bilingual Support — Funeral services available in both English and Spanish
  • 12+ Yrs Serving Families — Locally owned and operated
  • Flexible Arrangements — There is no requirement for an office visit

Funerals Your Way was shaped by years of listening to families through our ash scattering at sea services, where the need for clearer, more transparent, and more flexible funeral care became evident. From the beginning, our focus has been on providing families with guidance, dignity, and meaningful choices during difficult moments.

For more than 12 years, we have intentionally expanded our services to include cremation, burial, burial at sea, personalized memorial events, and comprehensive aftercare resources. Today, we combine traditional funeral values with modern options—allowing families to honor their loved ones in ways that feel personal, respectful, and true to their wishes.

Unique Services We Provide

Every family’s needs, values, and traditions are different. Our services are designed to offer flexibility, thoughtful options, and meaningful ways to honor a life—whether the gathering is simple or highly personalized.

Eco-Friendly Options

From biodegradable urns to sea burial and tree plantings, we help families choose sustainable memorials that give back to the earth.

Full Memorial Event Planning

Whether on land or at sea, our team assists with every detail, including catering, venues, music, and personalized touches.

Digital Aftercare Resources

We guide families through important post-funeral tasks such as obituary placement, Social Security notifications, and estate support.

Concierge Services for Traveling Families

With nearly one-third of our families coming from outside California, we specialize in making travel and arrangements seamless, ensuring every detail is taken care of.
