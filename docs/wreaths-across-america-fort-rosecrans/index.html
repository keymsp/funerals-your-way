Wreaths Across America at Fort Rosecrans National Cemetery

Wreaths Across America is a nonprofit organization dedicated to honoring the service and sacrifice of our nation’s veterans by placing remembrance wreaths on their graves during the holiday season. Founded in 2007 by Morrill Worcester of Maine, the organization has grown from a small tribute into a nationwide movement of remembrance.

Each year, Wreaths Across America coordinates wreath-laying ceremonies at more than 2,500 locations across the United States, including national cemeteries, memorials, and veterans’ monuments. Volunteers work alongside local communities, businesses, and veteran organizations to sponsor wreaths and carefully place them at each headstone, often speaking the veteran’s name aloud as a personal act of honor.

Guided by its mission to “Remember, Honor, Teach,” the organization not only remembers the fallen and honors those who served, but also educates future generations about the cost of freedom and the sacrifices made by military families. Through ceremonies, outreach programs, and community involvement, Wreaths Across America helps ensure that the legacy of our veterans endures.

The simple act of placing a wreath is both symbolic and powerful — a visible reminder that those who served are never forgotten.

Honoring Veterans in San Diego

The annual wreath-laying ceremony at Fort Rosecrans National Cemetery brings together families, veterans, and community volunteers to quietly place a wreath at each headstone and speak the veteran’s name aloud. The gesture is simple, yet deeply meaningful — a reminder that every service member is remembered as an individual.

Through the mission of Wreaths Across America, communities across the country participate in this act of remembrance, reinforcing the promise that those who served will never be forgotten. Here in San Diego, the ceremony reflects the strong military heritage of our region and the continued respect we hold for our veterans and their families.

Continuing the Commitment to Honor

While Wreaths Across America provides a meaningful annual tribute, honoring veterans is a responsibility that extends throughout the year. For families arranging services at Fort Rosecrans National Cemetery or other cemeteries in the region, ensuring proper military honors remains an important part of the farewell.
