San Diego Cremation With Memorial or Funeral Service

Cremation with a service offers families the opportunity to honor a loved one with intention, presence, and meaning — without the constraints of traditional burial. For many San Diego families, it provides the balance between simplicity and ceremony.

At Funerals Your Way, we coordinate professional cremation care along with a memorial service, funeral service, or celebration of life tailored to your family’s wishes. Services may be held before or after cremation and can take place at our funeral home, a place of worship, a private venue, or a meaningful outdoor setting.

Why Families Choose Cremation With a Service in San Diego

Many families want more than a basic cremation — they want time to gather, reflect, and honor a life in a meaningful way. Cremation with a service provides that opportunity, without the rigidity or expense of traditional burial.

  • Time to Say Goodbye Together — A service gives family and friends the opportunity to gather, share memories, and say goodbye in a meaningful way.
  • Shared Experience That Supports Healing — Coming together creates space for connection, reflection, and emotional support during a difficult time.
  • Flexible Timing and Location — Services can take place before or after cremation, and may be held at a funeral home, place of worship, or meaningful location.
  • Personalized, Meaningful Service — Each service can be tailored with music, readings, or traditions that reflect the life being honored.

There is no single way to hold a service — only what feels right for your family. We’re here to help guide you through the options with clarity and care.

A Service That Reflects the Life Being Honored

A cremation service can be simple or more traditional, depending on what feels right for your family. Services may take place before cremation as a funeral, or after as a memorial or celebration of life.

We help families create services that may include:

  • Memorial or funeral services
  • Celebration of life gatherings
  • Religious or non-religious ceremonies
  • Personalized music, readings, or tributes

We also assist families with religious and cultural traditions, including witness cremation for Hindu families, Buddhist ceremonies, and other customs, with respect for specific timing and practices.

Optional Enhancements & Personalization

Some families choose to include additional elements based on tradition, timing, or personal preference. These options allow you to further personalize the service in a way that feels meaningful.

  • Viewing or visitation prior to cremation
  • Clergy, celebrant, or officiant coordination
  • Customized urns, keepsakes, or printed materials
  • Music, readings, or visual tributes
  • Burial at sea or ash scattering arrangements
  • Embalming (typically required for viewing or visitation)

We explain each option clearly so you can decide what is meaningful for your family — without pressure or unnecessary additions.

Frequently Asked Questions About Cremation With a Service

Can we have a service before cremation?

Yes. Families may choose a funeral service with viewing or visitation prior to cremation, or hold a memorial service afterward.

Is embalming required?

Embalming is only required when viewing or visitation is selected. It is not required for cremation alone.

How soon does everything need to be decided?

There is no immediate rush. Cremation allows flexibility, and services may be held days, weeks, or even months later.

What is the difference between a funeral and a memorial service?

A funeral service typically takes place before cremation and may include viewing or visitation. A memorial service is held after cremation and focuses on remembrance and celebration of life.

Can we personalize the service?

Yes. Services can be personalized with music, readings, photos, video tributes, or cultural and religious elements that reflect your loved one’s life.

How much does cremation with a service cost?

Costs vary depending on the level of service, location, and personalization selected. We provide clear, upfront pricing so you understand exactly what to expect before making any decisions.
