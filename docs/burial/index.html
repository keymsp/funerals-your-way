Direct Burial (Unattended Interment) — $2,395

Some families choose direct burial, also known as unattended interment, because they prefer a simple and private burial without a ceremony. In this case, you would coordinate a burial with the cemetery, who then handle the burial without any family present.

This is often the most economical option, and is typically chosen by families who wish to memorialize their loved one alone or plan to have a memorial service in the future.

This option includes:

  • Transfer of the deceased into our care
  • Climate-controlled care prior to burial
  • Completion of required legal documentation
  • Transportation to the cemetery
  • Coordination with the cemetery
  • Disposition permit (1)
  • Aftercare package (complimentary)
  • Social Security notification
  • Tree planted in honor of your loved one (complimentary)

Not included: - Embalming - Viewing or ceremony - Casket or outer burial container (required by most cemeteries)

Burial with Cemetery (Graveside) Service — $3,195

Burial with a cemetery (graveside) service allows family and friends to come together at the cemetery for a brief service. This option allows them to be present for the interment without having to do a full funeral service at a church or other place.

This option is often selected by families who want a meaningful gathering at the cemetery while keeping arrangements simple and focused on the interment itself.

This option includes:

  • Transfer of the deceased into our care
  • Climate-controlled care prior to burial
  • Completion of required legal documentation
  • Transportation to the cemetery
  • Coordination with the cemetery
  • Disposition permit (1)
  • Aftercare package (complimentary)
  • Social Security notification
  • Tree planted in honor of your loved one (complimentary)

Not included: - Embalming - Viewing or ceremony - Casket or outer burial container (required by most cemeteries)

Traditional Burial with Service — $4,595

A traditional burial service permits people to come together before the burial. They have the option to choose a venue to host the service — be it a church, a chapel, a mortuary, or somewhere else. Then, the service will be followed by the burial at the cemetery. This option gives the participants time to think, remember, and be there for each other in a supportive, structured ceremony.

This option includes:

  • Transfer of the deceased into our care
  • Climate-controlled care prior to burial
  • Completion of required legal documentation
  • Embalming
  • Dressing, cosmetics, makeup
  • Hair
  • Directing service at venue and committal
  • Transfer to cemetery by hearse
  • Coordination with the cemetery
  • Disposition permit (1)
  • Aftercare package (complimentary)
  • Social Security notification
  • Tree planted in honor of your loved one (complimentary)

Not included: - Casket or outer burial container (required by most cemeteries)

Full Body Burial at Sea — $8,500 (around)

A full body burial at sea is a federally regulated alternative to traditional cemetery burial that allows a loved one to be laid to rest in the open ocean. This option is available to both civilian and military families and is conducted in compliance with all federal and environmental regulations.

Full body sea burials may be arranged as family-attended ceremonies or unattended committals, depending on the family’s wishes. This type of burial is often chosen by individuals with a deep connection to the ocean or those seeking a meaningful, non-cemetery burial option.

Frequently Asked Questions About Burial

Is choosing burial still common in San Diego?

Yes. Many families in San Diego continue to choose burial for religious, cultural, or personal reasons. Burial provides a permanent place of remembrance and a structured service that allows family and community to gather and honor a loved one.

What is the most affordable burial option you offer?

Our most affordable option is Direct (Unattended) Burial. This option includes professional care and cemetery transportation but does not include a service or family attendance.

What is the difference between direct burial and burial with a cemetery service?

Direct burial is unattended and scheduled by the cemetery without a service. A cemetery service burial includes a brief graveside committal where family members may be present, offering a simple but meaningful opportunity to gather.

Are your burial prices guaranteed and transparent?

Yes. We provide clear pricing upfront and review all options before any decisions are made. Families receive our General Price List and are never pressured to select services or merchandise they do not want.

Are you a licensed funeral home?

Yes. Funerals Your Way is a licensed California funeral home (FD #2188). All burial services are handled by licensed professionals, and a funeral director is available 24/7 to answer questions.

Does the burial package include the cemetery plot or fees?

No. Cemetery plots, opening and closing fees, and cemetery-required items are paid directly to the cemetery. We are happy to explain what to expect and help coordinate with the cemetery of your choice.

Is a casket required for burial?

Yes. A casket is required for burial. In addition, most cemeteries require an outer burial container or grave liner, which is not required by law but is typically a cemetery requirement. Exception can be made sometimes in the case of religious preferences, i.e., Islamic services.

Can families customize burial services?

Absolutely. While our packages cover common needs, families may customize services, add viewings, choose different merchandise, or adjust arrangements based on their preferences and traditions.

Do you help families choose a cemetery?

Yes. We work with all cemeteries throughout San Diego County and can help families compare options based on location, cost, religious affiliation, and availability.

Can arrangements be made without coming into the funeral home?

Yes. Many families complete arrangements by phone or online. In-person meetings are always welcome but never required.

How quickly can burial services be arranged?

Timing depends on documentation, cemetery scheduling, and family preferences. Once authorization is received, we coordinate promptly and keep families informed at every step.

What support is available after the burial?

Families receive access to our aftercare guidance, which includes help with death certificates, notifications, and next steps. Our support does not end when services are complete.
